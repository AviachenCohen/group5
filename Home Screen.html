How does stress and anxiety affect the skin of the face and body?

It is known that the skin is one of the organs through which it is possible to discover what is going on inside our body. Researchers have found that there is a direct and clear effect between stress and skin reaction. In the last few days when the stress and anxiety are increasing, some of us can already see some symptoms that have started to appear on the surface of the skin. For example, one of the chronic inflammatory skin diseases that break out in these times is seborrhea, about 15% of the population suffers from this common phenomenon.


With or without aluminum - is it really dangerous?

Medical studies show that aluminum can accumulate in the body and at the same time, there is no scientific evidence that directly links aluminum to certain health conditions, but in subjective cases of people suffering from serious chronic diseases. There are cases where the use of deodorant containing aluminum chloride may cause irritation. why is it happening? Because during use, the molecules that make up the aluminum are absorbed by the skin, which can cause the sweat glands to be blocked to the point of completely preventing sweating, which can cause irritation and allergy.


Sensitive and red skin

The facial area has thin, delicate skin that is exposed to the elements of the weather and the environment. Therefore, the skin tends to be particularly sensitive and dry out quickly. Redness in the facial skin is due to skin sensitivity and extreme dryness of the skin, which causes a reddish color in the skin, accompanied by facial skin irritation. Redness in the face can sometimes also be accompanied by peeling of the facial skin, which indicates that the skin is in distress and needs soothing and lots of moisture. Unprotected exposure to the sun and using products that are not suitable for your skin can also irritate the skin and cause redness.
